Who I Am

I'm Geoffrey Mumo — a Nairobi-based full-stack web developer, UI/UX designer, and technology trainer. I combine clean code with thoughtful design to build products that genuinely work for real Kenyan users.

Beyond development, I'm passionate about impact: I train communities in computer literacy, equipping individuals with the digital skills to access opportunities in Kenya's fast-growing tech economy.
